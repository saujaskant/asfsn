Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Anurag Society for Special Needs, Khatima, Uttarakhand

1. Who We Are

Anurag Society for Special Needs (ASFSN) is a registered society based in Khatima, Uttarakhand, India. Our website is located at anuragsocietyforspecialneeds.org. We are committed to protecting the privacy of everyone who visits our website or contacts us.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect information only when you voluntarily provide it to us through our contact form, newsletter subscription, or volunteer application. This may include:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone or WhatsApp number
  • Your message or query

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any analytics tools on this website. We do not collect any information automatically.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used only to:

  • Respond to your enquiry or message
  • Send you event updates and newsletters if you subscribed
  • Contact you regarding your volunteer application
  • Connect you with relevant resources or support

We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to any third party under any circumstances.

4. How We Store Your Information

Contact form and newsletter submissions are sent directly to our organisation's email address. We do not store form submissions on any external server. Your information is held securely and accessed only by authorised ASFSN staff.

5. Children's Privacy

Our website is designed for parents, educators, and the general public — not for children directly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are a parent sharing information about your child in the context of a support enquiry, that information is treated with the highest confidentiality and used only to provide the support you have requested.

6. Sensitive Information

We understand that information shared with us about autism diagnoses, therapy needs, or family circumstances is deeply personal and sensitive. We treat all such information with the utmost care and confidentiality. This information is never shared externally without your explicit written consent.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal information we hold about you
  • Request that we delete your information from our records
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time
  • Withdraw consent for us to contact you

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at +91 75059 08833 or through the contact form on our website.

8. External Links

Our website may contain links to external websites such as government portals, Facebook, and Instagram. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your information, please contact us:

  • Phone / WhatsApp: +91 75059 08833
  • Address: Anurag Society for Special Needs, Khatima, Uttarakhand, India
  • Website: anuragsocietyforspecialneeds.org
