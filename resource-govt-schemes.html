Financial Aid

Government Schemes & Financial Aid

Benefits, pensions, loans, and scholarships available to autistic individuals and their families in India

"Many families are entitled to significant government support — but don't know it exists. This guide changes that."

First Step — Get Your UDID Card

Before accessing most schemes, your child needs a Unique Disability ID (UDID) card. This is the official government proof of disability and is required for most benefits. Apply online at swavlambancard.gov.in or visit your District Disability Rehabilitation Centre (DDRC) in Udham Singh Nagar, Uttarakhand.

Central Government Schemes

🆔
Unique Disability ID (UDID) Card
Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

A single, unified disability identity card accepted across India. Required to access almost all disability benefits and reservations. Apply online or at your DDRC. Processing time is typically 30–60 days.

Apply at swavlambancard.gov.in →
💰
National Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation (NHFDC)
Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

Provides concessional loans to persons with disabilities for education, self-employment, and skill development. Loans are available at subsidised interest rates. Parents of autistic children can apply for education and therapy-related expenses.

Apply at nhfdc.nic.in →
🎓
National Scholarship for Students with Disabilities
Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment

Scholarships available for students with disabilities at pre-matric, post-matric, and top class levels. Autistic students with a valid UDID card are eligible to apply. Apply through the National Scholarship Portal.

Apply at scholarships.gov.in →
🏥
Ayushman Bharat – PM Jan Arogya Yojana
Ministry of Health & Family Welfare

Provides health insurance coverage of up to ₹5 lakh per family per year for hospitalisation. Disability-related treatments may be covered. Check eligibility at your nearest Common Service Centre or government hospital.

Uttarakhand State Schemes

💵
Divyang Pension Scheme (Uttarakhand)
Department of Social Welfare, Uttarakhand

Monthly pension for persons with disabilities with 40% or more disability. Autistic individuals with a UDID card and relevant certificate are eligible. Apply at your local Tehsil or District Social Welfare Office in Khatima or Udham Singh Nagar.

🚌
Free / Concessional Bus Pass
Uttarakhand Transport Corporation

Persons with disabilities holding a valid UDID card are entitled to free or heavily discounted travel on state-run buses. Apply at your nearest UTC depot with your UDID card and disability certificate.

How ASFSN Can Help

Navigating government paperwork can be confusing, especially when forms are only in Hindi or require multiple visits to different offices. ASFSN volunteers can help you identify which schemes you are eligible for, fill out application forms, and follow up with government offices.
