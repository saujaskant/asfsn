Autism in India — Real Conversations

Stories, guidance, and honest conversations with Indian families, therapists, and autistic adults — in Hindi and English

The ASFSN Podcast is a community-driven audio series that brings together the voices of parents, educators, therapists, and autistic individuals from across India. Every episode is rooted in real experience — not theory.

Episodes are available in both Hindi and English. New episodes are released monthly. Subscribe to our newsletter to be notified of new releases.
