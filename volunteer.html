Give your time, skills, or presence — and make a real difference for families in Khatima and beyond

"Volunteering with ASFSN doesn't require any special degree. It requires a willing heart and a few hours."

Why Volunteer?

Anurag Society for Special Needs runs entirely on community support. Our paid staff is small — our volunteer community is what makes our programs possible. Whether you can give one hour a month or one day a week, your contribution directly changes lives.

Volunteers gain: hands-on experience with autism support work, training in inclusive communication, a Certificate of Volunteering from ASFSN, and most importantly — the deep satisfaction of genuine community service.
