Anurag Society for Social Needs was established to be a community anchor for families navigating autism in India. We are not simply a school — we are a movement for awareness, acceptance, and access.

Our programs blend internationally recognised methodologies with deeply Indian values — understanding that the family is the first and most important teacher. We honour the RPWD Act, 2016 in every program we design.

Frequently Asked Questions
Autism is not a disease — it is a neurodevelopmental difference. It cannot and should not be 'cured.' The goal of support is to help autistic individuals live fulfilling, independent lives on their own terms, not to make them neurotypical.
Early intervention before age 5 has the greatest impact, but diagnosis is beneficial at any age. If you notice differences in communication, social interaction, or sensory responses, consult a developmental pediatrician. We can help connect you with diagnosticians across India.
Most community programs, awareness workshops, and parent resources are completely free. Some specialized training has a small nominal fee (₹200–500) to cover materials. No family is ever turned away due to financial constraints.
Yes. We provide resources in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Marathi, and Bengali. Our helpline has volunteers fluent in 8 Indian languages. Language should never be a barrier to getting support.
Fill the contact form below selecting 'Programs & Admissions' as your query type. One of our coordinators will call you within 24 hours to understand your child's needs and guide you through the process. No prior documentation is needed to make the initial enquiry.
